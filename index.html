Welcome To IIT Achalpur

Excellence guidance under the historical branches of topmost institute

IIT Achalpur, also known as the Indian Institute of Technology Achalpur, stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of education. Located in the historic city of Achalpur, this esteemed institution is renowned for its commitment to fostering innovation, academic rigor, and technological advancements. Established with a vision to nurture future leaders and pioneers in the fields of science and engineering, IIT Achalpur has consistently delivered top-tier education and cutting-edge research opportunities. The institution's distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a dynamic curriculum ensure that students receive a holistic and transformative learning experience. IIT Achalpur not only imparts knowledge but also instills a spirit of inquiry, creativity, and problem-solving in its students, making it a respected and coveted destination for those aspiring to excel in the world of science and technology.

Our Facilities

We provide you the best possible facilities in every way

World Class Library

IIT Achalpur's library is a remarkable hub of knowledge and resources that plays a pivotal role in supporting the institution's academic endeavors. The library boasts a vast collection of both print and digital resources, including textbooks, research journals, and reference materials, covering a wide array of subjects in science and engineering.

Largest Playground

IIT Achalpur boasts a well-equipped and expansive playground that spans approximately 5 acres and is meticulously maintained to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for students. It features state-of-the-art amenities, including well-marked cricket pitches, football fields, basketball courts, and tennis courts, catering to a wide range of sporting interests.

World Class Food

IIT Achalpur's cafeteria is renowned for offering a diverse and delectable range of food options that cater to the varied tastes and preferences of its student community. Students can savor freshly prepared meals, including vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, which are prepared with a strong emphasis on quality and hygiene.
